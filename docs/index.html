    How the model works

    A LightGBM regressor trained to minimise mean absolute error. A new model is promoted only if it beats the current champion by at least 2%.

    Algorithm LightGBM (gradient boosting)
    Trees 400
    Learning rate 0.05
    Promotion rule New MAE < Champion × 0.98
    Holdout Last 7 days
    Infrastructure Azure Container Apps

    What affects your delay?

    Time of day

    Rush hour, weekend vs weekday, public holidays, month of year, morning and evening peaks

    Route

    Line number, departure stop, transport mode — bus, metro, train, tram or ferry

    Weather

    Temperature, wind speed, rain, snowfall and cloud cover at departure time

    Recent history

    Average delay on this route in the last hour and last 24 hours
